# Retail Sales Business Analysis

A Business Analytics project demonstrating how SQL and Python can be used to transform raw retail transaction data into actionable business insights. This project focuses on sales performance, customer behavior, product profitability, regional trends, and management decision-making.

## 🎯 Project Objective

Retail businesses generate thousands of transactions every year. Simply looking at total sales is not enough to understand business performance.

This project answers important business questions such as:

- Which products generate the highest revenue?
- Which products are the most profitable?
- Which customers contribute the most sales?
- Which regions perform the best?
- How do discounts impact profitability?
- Are sales increasing over time?
- What seasonal trends exist?
