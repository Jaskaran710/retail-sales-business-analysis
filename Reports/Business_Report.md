\# Retail Sales Business Analysis Report



\## Executive Summary



The objective of this analysis was to evaluate retail sales performance, customer behavior, product performance, and profitability using SQL and Python.



\## Key Findings



\- Sales increased steadily from 2014–2017.

\- Strong sales seasonality was observed in Q4.

\- Technology generated the highest revenue.

\- Office Supplies produced consistent sales volume.

\- Higher discounts reduced profitability.

\- A small percentage of customers generated a large share of revenue.



\## Recommendations



\- Focus marketing on high-value customers.

\- Reduce excessive discounting.

\- Expand high-performing product categories.

\- Improve regional sales strategies.

\- Prepare inventory for seasonal demand.



\## Conclusion



The analysis provides data-driven insights to support business decisions related to sales optimization, customer retention, and profitability improvement.
